Our Story

At AYDE, we are dedicated to transforming communities through sustainable agricultural practices and innovative solutions. Since our establishment in 2013, we have been committed to offering high-quality seeds, organic farming education, and diverse agricultural products like teff, maize, pepper and other Products. Our commitment extends beyond agriculture to include construction, Comprehensive trade, financial services, and real estate. With a focus on ethical business practices, environmental sustainability, and community development, we strive to create lasting positive impacts. Join us in our journey to cultivate a prosperous and equitable future for all.

Unveiling Our Vision, Mission, and Core Values

Ayde is a dynamic organization dedicated to making a meaningful impact. With a bold vision and steadfast mission, the company is committed to establishing a profitable and ethical business that brings sustainable transformation to communities.

Vision

AYDE aspires to see opportunities fairly distributed in all corners and every society.

Mission

AYDE is committed to establishing a profitable and ethical business that brings sustainable change and transformation among society through the efforts of customers, business partners, and genuine participation of all stakeholders. The company focuses equally on social, environmental, and profit-driven goals

Objectives Of Our Company

AYDE Business Programs High Level Objectives

Economic Objectives


We focus on achieving sustainable profit though implementation of good and quality customer services.

Economic Objectives

AYDE focuses on achieving sustainable profit though implementation of good and quality customer services, providing reasonable prices, maintaining integrity and business accountability, and promotion of innovations and guarantee best possible use of resources.

Social Objectives


We will energetically work on social objective that will help to achieve for the benefit of the society.

Social Objectives

AYDE will energetically work on social objective that will help to achieve for the benefit of the society. Progressively, AYDE will commission to the maximum ceiling of 10% of its final profits for social and other related activities. At this specific strategic phase we strengthen our engagements on education, health, livelihood, and environment related development activities. Likewise, AYDE PLC. will also work to provide the society quality and good services with affordable prices; and fight against supply of adulterated or inferior good that can cause damages to customers and the society in general. We also adopt fair trade practices excluding black-marketing, hoarding disorders, over-charging and poor service delivery.

Human Objectives


We will work on ensuring economic, social and psychological well-beings of employees; and capacity development of small-holder farmers.

Human Objectives

AYDE will work on ensuring economic, social and psychological well-beings of employees; and capacity development of small-holder farmers. We focus on environmentally friendly models that will give due attention for environment enhancements and protection practices.
